Glycine

Key Stats

One Letter Code:
G
Molecular Forumula:
C2H5NO2
Molecular Weight:
75.07
Side-Chain Polarity:
Non-Polar

Information

Glycine has a single hydrogen atom as its side chain. It is the simplest possible amino acid. Glycine is one of the proteinogenic amino acids. Its codons are GGU, GGC, GGA, GGG of the genetic code.Glycine is a colorless, sweet-tasting crystalline solid. It is unique among the proteinogenic amino acids in that it is achiral. It can fit into hydrophilic or hydrophobic environments since it exists as zwitterion at natural pH, due to its minimal side chain of only one hydrogen atom.
